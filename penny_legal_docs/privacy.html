Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2024

Important: Penny is designed with privacy in mind. Your financial data stays on your device and is only synced through your personal iCloud account.

1. Information We Collect

Penny operates with a privacy-first approach:

  • Local Data: All your financial transactions, budgets, and categories are stored locally on your device
  • iCloud Sync: If enabled, your data is synced through Apple's iCloud service using your personal iCloud account
  • Analytics: We may collect anonymized usage analytics through Firebase to improve the app experience
  • No Account Required: Penny does not require you to create an account or provide personal information

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide core expense tracking functionality
  • To sync your data across your devices via iCloud (when enabled)
  • To improve app performance and user experience through analytics
  • To provide customer support when requested

3. Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: Your data is stored securely on your device using encrypted SQLite database
  • iCloud Sync: When enabled, data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account
  • No Third-Party Servers: We do not store your financial data on our servers
  • Biometric Security: Optional Face ID/Touch ID protection for app access

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal financial data with third parties. The only data sharing that occurs is:

  • Syncing to your personal iCloud account (when you enable this feature)
  • Anonymized analytics data to Firebase for app improvement

5. Your Rights

  • Data Control: You have full control over your data and can delete it at any time
  • Export: You can export your data in CSV or PDF format
  • Opt-out: You can disable iCloud sync and analytics at any time in settings

6. Third-Party Services

Penny uses the following third-party services:

  • Firebase Analytics: For anonymized usage analytics
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: For push notifications
  • Apple iCloud: For optional data synchronization
  • Google AdMob: For displaying ads in the free version

7. Children's Privacy

Penny is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date."

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@penny.app
